    About Me

    Hi! I am a senior at the University of Michigan School of Information studying User Experience Design with a minor in Computer Science.

    I began college as a Computer Science major and switched to the School of Information after discovering the UX Design track. My studies combine technology, business, and psychology to effectively research, design, and develop interactive products that connect businesses with their intended users. Usability testing, design theory, and iterative development help ensure that my prototypes function properly.

    Outside of class, I am an avid adventurer and love to travel. I spent last semester studying in Denmark, where I lived with an outdoor living & learning community. Some of my favorite moments included getting a backstage tour of the EU Parliament in Brussels, paragliding through Interlaken, and kayaking through the Danish canals.

    During the school year, I enjoy branching out from campus in my free time and building relationships outside of my college life. I work as a producer and lighting specialist on the tech team at my church where I help organize and execute live productions. I also volunteer at a local elementary school on Friday afternoons, where I create fun and engaging activities to strengthen the kids' reading and writing comprehension skills.

    I'm currently looking for full-time job opportunities involving UX design, web development, or quality assurance. Email me at laurelde@umich.edu if you'd like to chat, or connect with me using my social media links below!
